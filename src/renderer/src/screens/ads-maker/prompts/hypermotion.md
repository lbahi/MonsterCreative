# VIBE: HYPER MOTION
"Highlight Your Product"

## CREATIVE IDENTITY
Pure kinetic product showcase.
The product is the undisputed hero.
Every frame exists to make it look
extraordinary and immediately desirable.
Energy is relentless. Attention is demanded.
Nothing is passive. Nothing is safe.

Think: Apple product launch. Nike drop.
Red Bull highlight reel.

---

## PACING
Fast cuts. Nothing lingers except key reveals.
Each frame lands like a visual punch.
Speed ramps are encouraged on hero moments.
Motion blur on transitions is intentional.
The rhythm must feel beat-synced
even without hearing any music.

---

## CAMERA STYLE
Extreme close-ups dominate the grid.
Constant motion in every frame.
No static frames — except the end card.
The end card is always the final frame.
It is always static, clean, and branded.
This is the only exception to the motion rule.

Use:
  Unexpected angles — low, overhead, dutch tilt
  Orbit shots around the product
  Macro lens feel for surface and texture
  Speed ramp: fast → slow → fast on reveals
  The product rotates, pours, launches, or impacts
  It is always doing something

---

## COLOR AND LIGHT
High contrast. Punchy. Fully saturated.
Dark dramatic backgrounds preferred.
Product color pops against a muted world.
Hard directional lighting. Strong shadows.
Light leaks and lens flares are welcome.
Particle effects encouraged where natural:
  (water droplets, steam, dust, sparks, smoke)
  Only if they suit the product category.

---

## EXPECTED SHOTS — ALWAYS INCLUDE
In addition to the product category shots
identified in STEP 1 of base-rules, always include:

  Cold open: product fragment or abstract detail
  (viewer does not know what they are seeing yet)

  Full product reveal: dramatic, lit, impactful
  (the moment the product is fully visible)

  Hero macro: the most beautiful close-up
  of the product's single most compelling detail

  Speed ramp action: the most kinetic frame
  (fast → slow motion on the key product moment)

  End card: product static on dark background
  Brand name. Product name. Tagline.
  Clean. Minimal. Confident.

---

## AUDIO DIRECTION
High energy electronic or hip-hop beat.
Bass drop precisely synced to the hero reveal.
Sound design: material sounds, impacts,
mechanical clicks, whooshes, air release.
No voiceover required — visuals carry the story.
Music and sound design are the only voice.
