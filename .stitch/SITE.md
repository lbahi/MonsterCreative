# MosterAds — Site Vision

## 1. Overview
MosterAds is an enterprise AI-powered ad creative platform for Windows 11 desktop. It generates ad copy, images, and video ads at scale using brand DNA, enabling agencies to 10x creative output without increasing headcount.
